Our Business

RS-JED Machine Tools, Parts and Accessories Manufacturing is a machining and fabrication company that started out as a small workshop engaged in manufacturing of precision parts and sheet metal fabrication. In keeping abreast with the current global challenges, RS-JED upgraded its range of services and further raised its standard of quality to extend its expertise to companies in the region.

Our Goal

To exceed the expectations of every client by:

  • Outstanding customer service
  • Increased flexibility
  • On time delivery

We maintain a wide range of qualifications!

Our machinists are distinguished by their functional and technical expertise combined with their hands-on experience, thereby ensuring that our clients receive the highest quality of precision parts and sheet metal fabrications.

We excel in the Exceptional technical skills and unwavering commitment to our clients, coupled with extensive industry knowledge and experience makes RS-JED the ideal choice to provide service for all your Design and Fabrication needs.

Services

We offer the following services and manufacturing capabilities.

CNC Machining

Precision milling, turning, and post processing for metal and plastic parts. Prototypes and production.

3D Printing

Rapid SLS, SLA, MJF, and more. Choose from a variety of colors for any project type.

Sheet Metal Fabrication

Waterjet and laser cutting, bending, and popular post process finishes.
